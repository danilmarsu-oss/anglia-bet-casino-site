Understanding Problem Gambling

Problem gambling can affect anyone, regardless of age, background, or experience. It occurs when gambling stops being a fun and recreational activity and begins to have a negative impact on a person's life, finances, relationships, or mental health. At Angliabet Casino, we encourage all of our players to be aware of the warning signs of problem gambling, which may include:

  • Spending more money on gambling than you can afford to lose
  • Chasing losses in an attempt to win back money you have already lost
  • Gambling to escape stress, anxiety, or other emotional difficulties
  • Neglecting personal, professional, or family responsibilities due to gambling
  • Feeling irritable or restless when trying to cut back on gambling
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to fund gambling activities
  • Lying to friends or family members about the extent of your gambling

If you recognise any of these signs in yourself or someone you know, we strongly encourage you to make use of the tools and resources available on our platform and to seek professional help as soon as possible.
